Survey
  使用條件:(Conditions of usage)
    1. 照片需清晰並且有互相重疊的部分(images need  to be clear and Overlapping parts)
    2. 鏡頭須保持在一樣的水平高度(Cameras need to stay at the same level height)
    3. 鏡頭所拍攝的物體需為靜止狀態(object need to static state)

  元件選用:(Component select)
    1. EZCam PCM-512 (web camera)
    2. Raspberry Pi 3
